Racing is our bloodtype!

Simracing is our passion!

Simracing has its roots all the way in 1983 with what is believed to be the first racing sim called Chequered Flag by Psion on ZX Spectrum. There were some racing games prior this but Chequered Flag is considered the first Car Simulator.

"To finish first, you must first finish."

- Juan Manuel Fangio

“If you no longer go for a gap that exists, you're no longer a racing driver.”

- Ayrton Senna

Throughout the years, simracing is becoming more and more serious and simracers across the whole world are competing in many different competitions. Simracing saw a massive boom during early 2020 as whole world was put under lockdown due to Covid19. With majority of the real life comeptitions being on hold, virtual racing took the lead and for the first time, fans could watch e-racing in their own TVs.
